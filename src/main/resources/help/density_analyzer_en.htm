Density Analyzer

 

To quantify areas of increased or decreased density in the program the algorithm scans of numerical processing and histogram of gray values ​​along the transverse coordinates of the sample. "Analyser" button , which is present on the " tomograph " and on the " Model " shows the window in which the right side shows a tomogram with green bargraph coordinates . On the left side is a graph of the density of the sample in arbitrary units . Below the graph is a slider that changes when you move the analyzed transverse coordinate straight line along which the density measurement . It is possible to observe the displacement of the indicator line on the green image of the left.

Graphic allows you to zoom the chart area of ​​interest and store dates in the file system. The images below (Fig. 26 and Fig. 27 ) is shown with a different density analyzer scale density profile along the transverse coordinates of the sample. Easy to see that the area with reduced consumption clearly identified density chart . At the same time , of course, need to take into account the noise component is always present on the real tomograms , and apply additional noise filtering of the experimental data . Reducing the scanning step and the rotation step of the source- detector , it is possible to significantly improve the effectiveness of defect identification increased or decreased density of fuel rods.
